Co-Head Of Media, Volunteer

Hoa Phuong Do

As Co-Head of Media at Hoa Phuong Do, I helped coordinate and promote various community service activities, including clean-ups, fundraising, teaching assistance, and contests. My role was central in managing the media and outreach for the club’s impactful initiatives:


  • Organizing a Contest Day with dancing, physical games, and quizzes for over 110 club members, fostering team spirit and community engagement.
  • Filmed and edited videos for event recaps and promotions, reaching 2k followers on Facebook. contributing to regional recognition as one of the Top 100 Volunteer Club in VietNam
  • Aided in impactful campaigns like "Trade books for trees” which accumulated over 600kg (1320 pounds) of notebooks and paper, 1000 books, and many other donations towards children in need.
