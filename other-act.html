Tram Doc Cho Em - Charity Project

Media Team (June 2024 - September 2024)

I reached out to join this meaningful charity project which was aimed at raising funds for children in need in Son La, Vietnam. I was a member of the media team and my tasks include: Design and edit 10+ social media images and video;
Design a map for the event;
Design a standee and stickers for rewards;
Compiled and edited a book of heartfelt messages from over 100 volunteers across Vietnam.
My collaboration with the media team as well as teams from other sectors helped secure 6 sponsors, 11 media outlets, and 5 influencers, successfully raising over $6,000 and increasing the project's social media following by 3.5K+ on Facebook.
